# Freelance Developer Business Concept

## Core Identity

**What I do:** I build websites and web applications for clients who need digital solutions.

**How I work:** As a digital consultant and development partner - I understand the full business picture and can guide clients from concept to completion.

**Development approach:** Modern AI-assisted development (vibe coding) combined with full-stack capabilities.

---

## Service Offerings

### What I Build
- Landing pages and brochure websites
- E-commerce sites
- Custom web applications with databases
- Progressive Web Applications (PWAs)
- Business workflow automation
- Internal business applications

### What I Enjoy Most
Custom enterprise-style applications - building tailored solutions for specific business needs, workflows, and automation.

---

## Target Market

**Starting point:** Open to various client types while building experience
- Small businesses
- Startups
- Local companies
- Anyone who needs quality web development

**Long-term direction:** Will refine based on experience and what works best.

## Business Approach

### Positioning
Digital partner and consultant - not just a developer. I understand marketing, business needs, and the complete digital picture.

### Client Engagement
Flexible and client-focused:
- Project-based work
- Hourly rates
- Monthly retainers
- Ongoing support and maintenance
- Whatever makes sense for the client's needs

### Service Philosophy
- Be helpful and accessible
- Deliver quality work
- Build long-term relationships
- Stay flexible while building the business
